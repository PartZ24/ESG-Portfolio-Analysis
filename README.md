# ESG Portfolio Comparison with Sin Stocks Portfolio and Return-Weighted Average Comparison with ESG Mutual Funds
# Project Overview
This assignment involves a comprehensive comparative analysis of Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) investment strategies against traditional "sin stocks" portfolios, and benchmarking these custom portfolios against established ESG mutual funds in the Indian market. The analysis aims to evaluate whether ESG-focused investing can deliver competitive or superior risk-adjusted returns compared to conventional investments in controversial sectors.

# Analytical Framework
- **Phase 1 - Data Collection**: Stock prices, Nifty-50 benchmark, ESG scores from MSCI/Sustainalytics, mutual fund NAV/holdings, risk-free rates
- **Phase 2 - Portfolio Construction**: Equal-weight approach, rebalancing strategy definition, weight adjustments documentation
- **Phase 3 - Performance Calculation**: Monthly returns, variance/S.D., annualized metrics, CAPM application (Beta, Expected Return, Alpha)
- **Phase 4 - Comparative Analysis**: ESG vs sin stocks, mutual fund benchmarking, index comparison, risk-return trade-offs
- **Phase 5 - Statistical Testing**: T-tests for significance, Sharpe/Information/Sortino ratios

## Conclusion

This comprehensive assignment provides practical exploration of ESG investing principles through real-world portfolio management in the Indian market (2023-2024). By comparing an ESG-focused portfolio against a sin stocks portfolio and benchmarking both against established ESG mutual funds (ICICI ESG, Axis ESG) and the Nifty 100 ESG Index
